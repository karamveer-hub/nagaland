    About SDGCC

    The SDG Coordination Centre (SDGCC) under the Planning and Transformation Department, Government of Nagaland was established with technical assistance from the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) with a mandate to act as a centre for SDG knowledge dissemination, monitoring, policy strengthening and as an accelerator of SDG implementation across the State.
